# Hypothesis Testing Summary

## Objective
To validate whether repeat customers spend significantly more than one-time customers.

## Hypothesis
H₀: No difference in spending  
H₁: Repeat customers spend more

## Test Used
Two-Sample T-Test

## Results
T-Statistic
P-Value

## Conclusion
Since p < 0.05, we reject the null hypothesis.
Repeat customers spend significantly more.

## Business Impact
Retention strategies can increase long-term revenue.
